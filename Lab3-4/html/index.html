What is rICO?

Refunding

The invested funds can be returned within a month after the completion of the ICO without any conditions and without loss.

This mechanic is implemented in the code, you do not need to ask for anything, you simply send an empty transaction with a universal code to a smart contract and your investments are returned to your wallet immediately.

Project government

Each milestone of the project development is registered in the smart contract. All backers have the right to vote in acceptance or rejection of the next milestone of development.

If the majority of backers voted against the adoption of the milestone, then the next part of the financing will not be unlocked from the smart contract. This mechanism allows the backer to fully control the development process, up to a complete revocation of investments in the event that the team does not cope with the tasks in accordance with Roadmap.

rICO and the game

rICO participants receive tokens (ERC20), which can be automatically converted into the game currency after the game is launched.

The Game

CryptoFarmers is a combination of the most enticing gaming mechanics, implemented on the Ethereum blockchain. All game scenarios are programmed in smart contracts and the game is completely decentralized. The game currency is CryptoFarmers Ether (CFE).

Game mechanics

Players can purchase calves, pigs, kids, chickens and other pets. For pets it is necessary to look after, otherwise their quality will decrease.

Different pets give different useful goods. These goods can be sold to other players or exchanged for other goods.

Also pets can give offspring. In some cases a player can receive a pet with unique characteristics.

Advantages of rICO participants

pre-ICO

  • All pre-ICO participants get the opportunity to purchase zero-generation pets, as well as the maximum bonus on the amount of the game currency being purchased.
  • Pre-ICO smart contract is not Refundable!
  • Participants in the pre-ICO will have the same voting rights on the acceptance of the project milestones, as will rICO participants, but they will not have the opportunity to repay the investment.

rICO

  • All rICO participants will have the opportunity to purchase pets from the first generation. The number of pets of each generation is limited and will be mathematically calculated from the number of 0 generation pets, i.e. pre-ICO participants.

  • Also, the earliest participants of rICO will have the opportunity to receive 0 generation pets of non-purchased pre-ICO participants.
